BACKGROUND

The Agnetix Human Machine Interface (HMI) is included with the A3 lighting system and delivers visibility into a grow room’s health, providing growers with critical environmental and plant data.

Heat Recovery Technology

The Agnetix A3 platform significantly drives down operational costs with its unique liquid-cooling and heat recovery system. The system efficiently removes heat from the lights using a simple waterloop. Piped out of the room, the heat can be rejected or easily reused to warm the rest of the facility. Research on Race track

The factors that play an important role in track designing are as follows:
1) Clipping Point: It is a target on the race track edges which maximizes the chances of shortest possible route through the corners.
2) Race track Width: Width of the road is inversely proportional to the difficulty of the player. Width of the road is kept more for a long route, while width of the shortcut for the same route is kept small.
3) Curvature of turns: Angles between the turns should be chosen carefully. Difficulty of a turn increases if the angle between the turn is more acute.

Error Budgets and Developer Insight

Error Budgets: Error Budgets are designed to provide transparency into which services are not meeting their desired stability targets. This in turn helps guide investment towards improving stability of a given service. Error Budgets are computed based on a service or infrastructure defining its Service Level Target (SLT) for its uptime. Given a SLT, we can compute number of failures a service is allowed to have within a defined time frame. Stability Engineering built upon concepts of Error Budgets to create a tool for greater transparency and uniform monitoring across many of our services and key infrastructures.
